FACTS
WHAT DOES LINKEDIN DO WITH YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION?

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  INFORMATION YOU PROVIDE:
  - Name, email, phone number
  - Education, work experience, skills
  - Messages and posts

  INFORMATION COLLECTED AUTOMATICALLY:
  - Usage data (profile views, clicks)
  - Device and location data
  - Cookies and tracking data

  INFORMATION FROM THIRD PARTIES:
  - Employers and recruiters
  - Microsoft services
  - Contacts from other users

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
HOW IS YOUR INFORMATION USED?
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  - Build professional network
  - Provide job recommendations
  - Personalize content and ads
  - Improve AI systems and services

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
HOW IS YOUR INFORMATION SHARED?
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  - With other users (profile visibility)
  - With recruiters and employers
  - With service providers
  - For legal purposes

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
PRIVACY CONTROLS:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  - Control profile visibility
  - Edit or delete information
  - Manage ad preferences

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
IMPORTANT NOTES:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  - Some data may be publicly visible
  - Data may come from other users or partners
