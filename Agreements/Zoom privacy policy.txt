FACTS
WHAT DOES ZOOM DO WITH YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION?

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  INFORMATION YOU PROVIDE:
  - Name, email, account details
  - Meeting registration data

  INFORMATION COLLECTED AUTOMATICALLY:
  - Device data (IP, OS, hardware)
  - Usage data (meeting activity)
  - Audio, video, chat content (meeting data)

  INFORMATION FROM THIRD PARTIES:
  - Integrated apps (calendar, email)
  - Business partners

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
HOW IS YOUR INFORMATION USED?
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  - Provide video conferencing services
  - Improve product performance
  - Ensure security and prevent fraud
  - Customer support

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
HOW IS YOUR INFORMATION SHARED?
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  - With meeting hosts and participants
  - With service providers
  - With third-party integrations
  - For legal purposes

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
PRIVACY CONTROLS:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  - Manage meeting settings
  - Control recordings
  - Adjust account privacy settings

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
IMPORTANT NOTES:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  - Meeting hosts can record sessions
  - Zoom does not use meeting content for AI training
