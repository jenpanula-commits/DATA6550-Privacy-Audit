FACTS
WHAT DOES UBER DO WITH YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION?

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  INFORMATION YOU PROVIDE:
  - Name, phone number, email
  - Payment information
  - Profile details

  INFORMATION COLLECTED AUTOMATICALLY:
  - Real-time GPS location
  - Trip history (pickup, drop, time)
  - Device and usage data
  - Communication data (calls, messages)

  INFORMATION FROM THIRD PARTIES:
  - Business partners
  - Identity verification services
  - Marketing partners

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
HOW IS YOUR INFORMATION USED?
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  - Match riders with drivers
  - Track trips and navigation
  - Process payments
  - Improve safety and prevent fraud
  - Personalize services

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
HOW IS YOUR INFORMATION SHARED?
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  - With drivers and delivery partners
  - With service providers
  - With law enforcement if required
  - With business partners

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
PRIVACY CONTROLS:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  - Control location permissions
  - Access and delete data
  - Manage account settings

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
IMPORTANT NOTES:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  - Uber collects continuous location data
  - May use biometric verification (face ID)
