FACTS
WHAT DOES META DO WITH YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION?
Rev. 2024–2025

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
WHY?
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Meta builds products — including Facebook, Instagram, Messenger, and Threads —
that help people connect with friends, family, communities, and businesses. To
provide these services and keep them free to users, Meta relies on advertising
revenue, which requires collecting and using personal information to serve
relevant ads. This Privacy Policy explains what information Meta collects,
how it is used and shared, and what rights and controls you have.

This policy applies to: Facebook, Instagram, Messenger, and Threads.
This policy does NOT cover: WhatsApp, Workplace, Messenger Kids, Quest devices
(without a Facebook account), or Free Basics (each has its own privacy policy).

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The types of personal information Meta collects depend on how you use its
products. This information can include:

  INFORMATION YOU PROVIDE DIRECTLY:
  - Name, email address, phone number, date of birth, gender, and profile photo
  - Relationship status, education, employment history, and interests (profile)
  - Posts, photos, videos, Stories, Reels, and comments you create or share
  - Messages and communications (metadata collected; content encrypted in some
    products; private messages with friends/family excluded from AI training)
  - Payment information (for Facebook/Instagram shopping features)

  INFORMATION COLLECTED AUTOMATICALLY:
  - Behavioral data: likes, reactions, shares, viewing time, scroll behavior,
    ad engagement actions, and content interactions
  - Off-platform data: browsing and purchase behavior on third-party websites
    and apps via the Meta Pixel, Conversions API, and Social Plugins
    (collected even if you do not have a Facebook/Instagram account)
  - Device identifiers, hardware specs, OS version, and app version
  - Wi-Fi network, Bluetooth signals, IP address, and mobile network data
  - Location data: GPS (if permitted), IP-based inference, and location tags
  - Cookie data from devices and browsers across Meta's services

  INFERRED / DERIVED DATA:
  - Interest categories, behavioral profiles, and inferred sensitive attributes
    (which may include political views, religious beliefs, socioeconomic status)
    used to build advertising profiles — even from off-platform activity

  INFORMATION FROM THIRD PARTIES:
  - Advertisers, app developers, and publishers (via Meta Business Tools, APIs,
    SDKs, and the Meta Pixel)
  - Other Meta Companies (Instagram, WhatsApp, Messenger) per their own terms
  - Third-party data brokers and partners who have rights to share with Meta

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
HOW DOES META USE THIS INFORMATION?
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Meta uses collected information for the following purposes:

  - Personalize your experience (content rankings, friend suggestions, ads)
  - Provide and improve Meta Products (research, testing, troubleshooting)
  - Show targeted advertising based on your profile, behavior, and interests
    (Note: Meta was ruled by the CJEU in 2023 to be unable to rely on
    "legitimate interest" as a legal basis for behavioral advertising in the EU)
  - Support AI at Meta (including AI-generated content and AI features;
    user-shared content such as posts and photos may be used; private messages
    with friends/family are excluded from AI training)
  - Promote safety, security, and integrity across Meta products
  - Conduct research and surveys about Meta products and services
  - Communicate with you about policy changes, security, and features

TO LIMIT SHARING / PRIVACY CONTROLS:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  * Ad Preferences         – facebook.com/ads/preferences
  * Off-Facebook Activity  – facebook.com/off_facebook_activity
    (disconnect future off-platform activity; does not delete historical data)
  * Download Your Info     – facebook.com/dyi (access or download your data)

HOW DOES META PROTECT YOUR INFORMATION?
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  - Encryption in transit and at rest for many product features
  - Two-factor authentication (strongly encouraged, available for all accounts)
  - Automated abuse detection and account integrity systems
  - Dedicated security and trust & safety teams
  - Security Help Center: https://www.facebook.com/help/security

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
HOW LONG IS YOUR INFORMATION RETAINED?
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Meta retains personal information for as long as it is necessary to provide
products and services and to comply with legal obligations. Deleted account data
is processed within a 30-day grace period (during which you can cancel deletion)
followed by a 90-day technical deletion window. Some data may be retained longer
for legal compliance, safety investigations, or regulatory requirements.

Content you've shared (messages to others, posts others have reacted to) may
remain in others' accounts even after you delete your account.
