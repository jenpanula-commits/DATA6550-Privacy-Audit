FACTS
WHAT DOES NETFLIX DO WITH YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION?
Rev. November 2023 / Updated 2024

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
WHY?
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Netflix is an internet television streaming service offering movies, TV shows,
documentaries, and other content. When you sign up for a Netflix membership,
you provide personal information to create your account and access the service.
Netflix collects and uses this information to deliver, personalize, and improve
the streaming experience, to process payments, and — for members on the
ad-supported plan — to serve relevant advertisements through its ad technology
partner, Microsoft Advertising.

This Privacy Statement explains how Netflix collects, uses, discloses, and
protects your personal information, and what rights and choices you have.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The types of personal information Netflix collects depend on your membership
plan and how you use the service. This information can include:

  ACCOUNT AND SUBSCRIPTION DATA:
  - Name, email address, postal/ZIP code, and phone number
  - Password and account credentials
  - Subscription tier (ad-supported or standard/premium)
  - Payment method information: credit/debit card number, expiration date,
    billing address (processed via third-party payment partners)

  VIEWING AND USAGE DATA:
  - Titles watched, searched for, and browsed within Netflix
  - Playback behavior: completion percentage, pause points, rewind events,
    and time/date of viewing
  - Ratings, reviews, and content preferences you provide
  - User interface navigation and how you browse the content catalog
  - Search history within the Netflix platform

  DEVICE AND TECHNICAL DATA:
  - IP address (used to infer general location and for security purposes)
  - Device type (TV, phone, tablet, computer), device identifiers
  - Browser type, operating system, and screen resolution
  - Network provider and connection speed
  - Application version and crash/diagnostic data

  MARKETING AND AD DATA (Ad-Supported Plan):
  - Viewing context and content categories viewed (for ad targeting)
  - Ad interaction data (whether you viewed, paused, or skipped an ad)
  - Unique identifiers provided by advertisers (cookies, device identifiers)
  - Demographic and interest information from advertising partners

  INFORMATION FROM THIRD PARTIES:
  - Advertising Companies: Advertisers on Netflix may provide unique identifiers
    and demographic data to support ad delivery and measurement
  - Partners (smart TV manufacturers, ISPs, device platforms) if you access
    Netflix through a partner integration
  - Publicly available social media sources (where you have tagged Netflix
    publicly)

  INFORMATION COLLECTED USING COOKIES AND TRACKING TECHNOLOGIES:
  - Cookies, pixel tags, and web beacons used to recognize your device,
    maintain session information, and support analytics and advertising

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
HOW DOES NETFLIX USE THIS INFORMATION?
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Netflix uses collected information for the following purposes:

  - Provide and deliver the Netflix streaming service and process payments
  - Personalize your experience: content recommendations based on viewing
    history, ratings, and browsing behavior
  - Analyze and improve the service: research, testing, and performance analysis
  - Communicate with you: service updates, account notifications, and marketing
    emails (you can opt out of marketing emails at any time)
  - Support content acquisition: aggregate viewing data may inform decisions
    about which content to license or produce (in de-identified form to studios)
  - Serve advertising (ad-supported plan): deliver targeted ads through
    Microsoft Advertising using viewing context and audience data
  - Protect the service: detect fraud, abuse, and unauthorized account sharing

HOW DOES NETFLIX PROTECT YOUR INFORMATION?
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  - Reasonable administrative, logical, physical, and managerial security
    measures to safeguard information against loss, theft, and unauthorized access
  - Encryption of payment data via PCI-DSS compliant payment processors
  - Secure account authentication with optional two-factor authentication
  - Regular internal security reviews and third-party security assessments

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
HOW LONG IS YOUR INFORMATION RETAINED?
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Netflix retains personal information for as long as necessary to provide the
service and fulfill legal, contractual, or business obligations. After account
deletion, most personal data is deleted or de-identified within a reasonable
technical window, subject to:
  - Legal or regulatory retention requirements (e.g., financial records)
  - Ongoing fraud investigations or security reviews
  - Contractual obligations to content partners

Viewing history and account activity data is retained for as long as your
account is active and for a period after deletion as required by law.

OTHER IMPORTANT INFORMATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

    Netflix at privacy@netflix.com.

  EU/EEA/UK Residents (GDPR/UK GDPR):
    Netflix relies on Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) for international data
    transfers. You have rights to access, erasure, portability, restriction, and
    objection. Contact: privacy@netflix.com or your national Data Protection
    Authority. No major GDPR enforcement actions have been taken against Netflix
    as of the date of this document.

  Children:
    Netflix is not intended for children under 13 without parental supervision.
    Netflix Kids profiles restrict content to age-appropriate titles.
