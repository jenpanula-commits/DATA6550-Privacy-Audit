FACTS
WHAT DOES GOOGLE DO WITH YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION?
Rev. April 2026

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
WHY?
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Google builds a range of services — including Search, Gmail, YouTube, Maps,
Chrome, Android, and Google Home — that help millions of people daily. When you
use these services, you trust Google with your information. This Privacy Policy
explains what information is collected, why it is collected, and how you can
update, manage, export, and delete your information.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The types of personal information Google collects depend on how you use its
services. This information can include:

  INFORMATION YOU PROVIDE DIRECTLY:
  - Name, email address, password, and phone number (when creating an account)
  - Payment information (for Google Pay, Play Store, and other purchases)
  - Content you create, upload, or receive: emails, photos, documents, comments
  - Contact information from your address book or communications

  INFORMATION COLLECTED AUTOMATICALLY:
  - Device identifiers, browser type, operating system, and application version
  - IP address, mobile network information, and crash reports
  - Search terms, videos watched, voice and audio information (Google Assistant)
  - Location data: GPS, Wi-Fi access points, cell towers, Bluetooth signals
  - Activity on third-party sites and apps that use Google services (ads,
    analytics, Google Sign-In, reCAPTCHA, embedded Maps)
  - Chrome browsing history synced with your Google Account
  - Purchase activity and calling/messaging logs (Google services)

  INFORMATION FROM THIRD PARTIES:
  - Directory services and marketing partners (business information for ads)
  - Security partners (data to protect against abuse and fraud)
  - Advertisers and publishers (ad performance and audience data)
  - Publicly accessible sources (e.g., local news indexed by Google Search)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
HOW DOES GOOGLE USE THIS INFORMATION?
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Google uses collected information for the following purposes:

  - Provide services (process searches, deliver emails, enable Maps navigation)
  - Maintain and improve services (troubleshoot issues, fix outages)
  - Develop new services (using insights from existing products)
  - Personalize content and advertising (recommendations, customized ads via
    My Ad Center; Google does NOT personalize ads based on sensitive categories
    such as race, religion, sexual orientation, or health)
  - Measure performance (analytics for Google services and advertisers)
  - Communicate with you (security alerts, service announcements)
  - Protect Google, users, and the public (fraud prevention, spam detection)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
TO LIMIT SHARING / PRIVACY CONTROLS:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Google provides several tools to manage your privacy:

  * My Ad Center         – myadcenter.google.com  (manage ad personalization)
  * Google Takeout       – takeout.google.com     (download / export your data)
  * My Activity          – myactivity.google.com  (review or delete activity)
  * Privacy Checkup      – myaccount.google.com/privacycheckup
  * Location controls    – Device Settings > Location (Android/iOS)
  * Web & App Activity   – Account Settings > Data & Privacy

  Please note: Turning off personalization may limit features but does not stop
  all data collection. Base logging, security, and service data are always
  collected. Your choices apply to your account across all devices signed in.

  For questions: https://support.google.com/policies

HOW DOES GOOGLE PROTECT YOUR INFORMATION?
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Google uses industry-standard security measures including:
  - Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  - Two-Step Verification and Security Checkup tools
  - Hundreds of millions of dollars invested annually in security infrastructure
  - World-renowned security expert teams dedicated to data protection
  - Regular security reviews and audits of internal systems

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
HOW LONG IS YOUR INFORMATION RETAINED?
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Retention periods vary by data type and are described in detail at:
https://policies.google.com/privacy#inforetaining

  - Some data (e.g., search history) is auto-deleted after 18 months by default
  - Activity controls can be set to delete automatically after 3 or 18 months
  - Account data is retained as long as your account exists
  - Some data may be retained longer for legal, safety, or business reasons
