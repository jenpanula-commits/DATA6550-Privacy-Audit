FACTS
WHAT DOES SNAPCHAT DO WITH YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION?

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Snapchat collects the following types of personal information:

  INFORMATION YOU PROVIDE:
  - Name, username, email, phone number, birthday
  - Profile picture, Bitmoji
  - Content such as photos, videos, chats, and messages

  INFORMATION COLLECTED AUTOMATICALLY:
  - Usage data (stories viewed, filters used, interactions)
  - Device data (IP address, OS, browser, device sensors)
  - Location data (GPS, IP-based location)
  - Metadata (time, date, engagement)

  INFORMATION FROM THIRD PARTIES:
  - Advertisers and partners
  - Contact lists from other users
  - Linked apps and services

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
HOW IS YOUR INFORMATION USED?
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  - Provide and maintain services
  - Personalize content and advertisements
  - Recommend friends and content
  - Improve app features and AI systems
  - Ensure security and prevent fraud

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
HOW IS YOUR INFORMATION SHARED?
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  - With service providers and partners
  - With advertisers (personalized ads)
  - For legal and regulatory purposes
  - With other users based on your privacy settings

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
PRIVACY CONTROLS:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  - Access and update your data
  - Delete your account and content
  - Control who sees your content
  - Download your data
  - Opt out of ads

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
IMPORTANT NOTES:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  - Messages may disappear but can be saved or screenshotted
  - Snapchat collects behavioral and location data
